Our Story

The Houston soul of every great event.

Password has been Houston's open-secret for a generation of brides, fundraisers, and Fortune 500 holiday parties — a high-energy show band built on real chops, relentless rehearsal, and a setlist your guests already know by heart.

We are a variety band committed to delivering excellence in high-energy performance with audience-interactive engagement. We offer a musical experience in (but not limited to) R&B, Top 40, Classic Rock, Motown, Disco, and Romantic Favorites — a setlist built to read any room from a 200-person Houston wedding to a 2,000-seat gala.

We are available to those of you looking to partner with quality entertainment for your special event — weddings, debutante balls, galas, corporate parties, festivals, and other private events across Houston, Galveston, Sugar Land, The Woodlands and all of Texas.

Follow our social media to keep up with the many memorable experiences we share — we’ll be sure to give you a shout out.
